Aswang

Boo! It's one of the worst (or best?) creatures in the night time stories that your mother would tell you so you will not stay up late and be a good child who stays at home when the sun set.

Made in a creepy charcoal texture artstyle, A childhood fear is expressed through this art. An evil spirit from the filipino folklore called a “Tikbalang” is feared among children whenever they roam around trees.

A special thanks to Mr. Jonathan Zachary Kyle Uson for giving us a reminder of the goosebumps we felt as a child because of the stories that we used to hear. He used IbisPaintX and a technique of digital art while giving us the illusion of charcoal being used because of its texture.
